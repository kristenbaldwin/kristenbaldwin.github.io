I developed an interest in learning how to create high converting sites and became more involved with my dev team, from logging bugs to testing fixes and new releases. To become a software developer, I decided to attend a 16 week immersive coding bootcamp with Digital Crafts. While attending Digital Crafts, I discovered a passion for front-end development and am looking forward to helping you with your web development needs.
