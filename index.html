About Me

As a graduate of The University of Texas at Austin, I began my career in digital marketing. From working at an agency to e-commerce, I honed my marketing skills in email, social media, advertising, site management and analysis. I developed an interest in learning how to create high converting sites and became more involved with my dev team, from logging bugs to testing fixes and new releases. To become a software developer, I decided to attend a 16-week immersive coding bootcamp with DigitalCrafts. While attending DigitalCrafts, I discovered a passion for front-end development and am looking forward to helping you with your web development needs.
